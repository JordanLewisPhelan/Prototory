
 ***What is the Games Goal?*** 
 
**Technically :**
In short the game will be a simple orthographic top down game so that visuals are distinct but not a priority. It will show off interconnected systems and the ability to micro & macro manage a chain of different systems while still having them distinct.
These systems, will moving different minerals or resources - some may be unable to be moved the same as others, this will be the beginning or the players choices.

**Gameplay :**
The gameplay(This will discuss the background too, need to have a direction to layout this long term);
Will consist of being an early terraformer on recently discovered world can sustain life, so you are tasked with setting up the initial foundation work with the assistance of a Company Satellite which will drop in supplies when the Station between this planet and the Satellite recieve the resources necessary to construct these devices. The Goal is making a minimally viable environment for people to live, so to meet these ends;
You will need to get your hands dirty and make rudimentary tools with the supplies you are given when you land and Using your ingenuity and the Satellite and Space Station crews help, construct a larger self sufficient system so that you can process enough materials and minerals to build all the services and machines.

This could be biodomes, Communication Relays, etc.
As you progress your advancements will contribute to the Space Station Crew having more to work with, giving you blueprints for more content.
Some will be quite expensive and non-necessary but vital to you as a player in terms of expansion and getting to make the Place more hospitable.
So to this end you will not want to be mining and carrying water back and forth, so you have been sent down with schematics and pre-packaged devices that will help automate this process - this is where player ingenuity will thrive as things that are further from your main hub area wherever you set that up will be helpful, but will take much longer to reach and as such  process.


  ***Game Progression***

The games flow should in my mind be systemic and exponential in growth.
All players will start, with basic items or be a part of their kit no item physically required - still ironing minor details like that out. - But as a result its basic, youll have to manually gather these resources and  this will make automating the process feel nice initially. Always.
	NOTE: Perhaps forcing players to manually move each new resource or item and get a Codex of a substance to see how it works and fairs chemically before they can be automated would be a nice idea? That feeling of earning the rite to passive income.

Following this we will open up more schematics and this will forward the player into a new part of the game - Creating an automation bridge between a source of a mineral/resource and the Base.
Some resources (Should be?)(are?) limited, so maybe the player can gain enough, to have a Smart Miner(TM) which once the Codex has enough samples will generate a reusable item for some items to connect them from a Source to the Base, this would be an earned skill and prevent intense monotony. No one wants to construct several hundred resource lines in all directions to farm a resource.
	NOTE: Granted, allowing the end of the process to be removed and extended might suit, only remove the miner and allow player to bridge onto a new Line that feeds back into the main system, might be better - Yes no it might be.

Player Will control where the direction of where a funnel will flow, this way it is easier to program and gives player agency to try some wacky inventions - perhaps a Speed machine? Pipelines or rather Conveyors could be mulitpurpose and applies a velocity which player can use as a movement system.. getting a little too technical but its fun to brainstorm. 

But this covers the progression and flow, it is not up to me as the Developer to choose the Players path, they work with what they have to make the world their own. To mold it to be as optimal or as grindy as they so see fit, at their own Pace.
